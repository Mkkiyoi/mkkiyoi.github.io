Who am I?

Hi! My name is Matthew Kiyoi. I currently work at Costco Wholsale as a front end assistant and am a former Campus Minister with InterVarsity Christian Fellowship at the University of Washington, Seattle. As a Californian I constantly miss the sun, good Mexican food (please, tell me I'm wrong...), and wear sandals everywhere. When I am not working on a web development project or learning new technologies, I love to read, cook, bake, and play Riot's new game Teamfight Tactics.

I am also passionate about relationships. The way to have deep friendships and great partnerships with coworkers is through having meaningful conversations with these people. Although I personally hate small talk, I love to listen to people's stories and even debate about this like politics or fiath.
